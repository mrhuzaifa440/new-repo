Yes, there are global guidelines to address the environmental impact of electricity generation, including greenhouse gas standards and regulations promoting the use of renewable energy sources.

How does electricity generation impact the environment?

Electricity generation can impact the environment through emissions of greenhouse gases, air pollutants, and the depletion of natural resources. Transitioning to renewable energy sources can help mitigate these impacts.

What is the power sector evolution?

The power sector evolution refers to the changes and advancements in electricity generation technologies, policies, and market dynamics over time. It involves a shift towards cleaner and more sustainable energy sources.

What is the current power generation capacity globally?

The global power generation capacity refers to the total amount of electricity that can be produced by power plants. It varies across countries and regions based on their energy infrastructure and resources.

Yes, major shifts are predicted in global electricity generation, including an increased share of renewable energy, advancements in energy storage technologies, and the phasing out of fossil fuel-based power plants.
