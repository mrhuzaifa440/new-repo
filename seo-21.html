what is seo?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a crucial aspect of digital marketing that aims to improve a website's visibility and ranking on search engine results pages. It involves utilizing strategic techniques and best practices to ensure that a website is optimized for search engines. By doing so, SEO helps attract organic traffic, increase brand visibility, and improve the overall online presence of a business or organization.

When it comes to SEO, keyword research plays a fundamental role. Keywords are specific words or phrases that users enter into search engines when looking for information or solutions to their queries. By identifying and selecting relevant keywords related to a website's content, SEO professionals can optimize the website's pages to appear higher in search engine rankings. This, in turn, increases the chances of attracting more qualified traffic and potential customers. Keyword research involves analyzing search volume, competition, and user intent to determine the most effective keywords to target in SEO strategies.
