Society and Culture: The Pulse of Pakistan

Pakistan is a country with a rich and diverse society and culture. One of the key aspects that defines the pulse of Pakistan is its wedding traditions and festivals. Weddings in Pakistan are grand and elaborate affairs, filled with colors, music, and traditions that reflect the country's cultural heritage. From the Mehndi ceremony, where intricate henna designs are applied to the bride's hands, to the Baraat, where the groom arrives on a decorated horse, each wedding ceremony is a celebration of love and family. Festivals such as Eid, Basant, and Pakistan Day are also important cultural events that bring people together to celebrate and strengthen their bonds. Through these traditions and festivals, the people of Pakistan showcase their deep-rooted cultural values and traditions, making society and culture an integral part of the country's identity.
