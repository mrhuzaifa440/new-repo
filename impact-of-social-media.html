Such acts transpire within both private and public realms online, rendering escape from this torment an arduous endeavor for its unfortunate targets. While social media platforms have endeavored to combat cyberbullying and online harassment by implementing measures aimed at mitigation, the sheer magnitude and intricacy inherent in these expansive virtual domains render complete eradication an elusive ambition indeed.
